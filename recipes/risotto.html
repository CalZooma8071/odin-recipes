Ingredients

  • Arborio rice
  • Mushrooms(fresh or dried )
  • Broth(chicken or vegetable)
  • White Wine
  • Butter and Olive oil
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Herbs

Steps

  1. Toast the Rice - Lightly in the pan
  2. Add White Wine - Rice to release starches and adds flavor
  3. Slowly Add Warm Broth - For creamy texture
  4. Stir Often - helps to release starches and creates a creamy texture
  5. Add Flavorings - Garlic,parmesan cheese,butter,herbs
  6. Rest the Risotto for a few minutes - Allows the flavors and texture to settle
